02. Our Development Roadmap

The core ecosystem focuses on centralizing Ocean Frontiers' data and eliminating the need for generic third-party subscriptions.

  • Data Consolidation: Migrating all our disparate spreadsheets into one "Single Source of Truth."
  • Integrated Messaging: Native emailing of waivers, dive logs, and schedule changes directly to our guests.
  • 55-Dive Challenge: Automated tracking for one of our core guest engagement milestones.
  • Complete POS Integration: Replacing Vend with a system that "talks" to our booking database for seamless invoicing.
  • B2B Inventory Hub: Allowing our partner agents to book live boat capacity in real-time.

Note: The Guest Mobile Application (Phase 4) is a separate architectural undertaking and is not included in this foundational license.
